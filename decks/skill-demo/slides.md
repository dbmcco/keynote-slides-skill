## Headline Rules Applied
- Image & Action: concrete nouns + strong verbs (flipped, vanish, ship, adapts, generates, challenge)
- Tension & Turn: "not thinking" / "not a blank slide"
- Cadence: 6-10 words each
- Power verbs: flipped, vanish, ship, generates, adapts, structure, challenge
- Metaphor family: workflow/journey (ship, build, starts)
